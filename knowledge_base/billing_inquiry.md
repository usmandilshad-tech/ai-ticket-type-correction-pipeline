# Billing Inquiry

This category covers questions and problems involving payments, charges, invoices, billing statements, payment methods, card transactions, declined payments, duplicate charges, billing addresses, and subscription charges.

Examples include:

- A payment was declined.
- A customer does not recognize a charge.
- The customer was charged twice.
- An invoice is missing or incorrect.
- The customer wants to update payment details.
- A card transaction failed.
- The customer has a question about a billing statement.

Do not use this category when the customer explicitly asks for money back. An explicit request for repayment should normally be classified as a refund request.
