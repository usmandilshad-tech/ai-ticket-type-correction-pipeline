# Refund Request

This category covers explicit requests for money to be returned, reimbursed, or refunded.

Examples include:

- I want a refund.
- Please return my money.
- I need reimbursement.
- What is the status of my refund?
- I cancelled and want my payment returned.
- I was charged incorrectly and want my money back.
- The product was defective and I want a refund.

The reason for the request may involve billing, cancellation, delivery, or a technical problem, but the requested outcome is repayment.
