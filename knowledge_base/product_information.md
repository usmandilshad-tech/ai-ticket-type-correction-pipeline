# Product Information Guide

## Product Inquiry Handling

Customers may contact support to ask about product features, compatibility, setup, specifications, pricing, availability, or usage instructions.

## Feature Questions

If a customer asks about product features:

1. Identify the product name.
2. Provide a concise explanation of the relevant features.
3. Share setup or usage guidance if needed.
4. Offer to connect the customer with a specialist if the question is highly technical.

## Compatibility Questions

If a customer asks whether a product is compatible with another device or system:

1. Ask for the exact product model.
2. Ask for the device, software, or platform they want to use it with.
3. Check compatibility information.
4. If compatibility is uncertain, escalate to technical support.

## Setup Guidance

If a customer needs help setting up a product:

1. Confirm the product name and model.
2. Ask which step they are stuck on.
3. Provide step-by-step guidance.
4. If setup fails, move to technical troubleshooting.

## Suggested Support Tone

Be helpful, informative, and clear. Product inquiries are often pre-sale or early-use questions, so the response should be easy to understand.
