# Billing FAQ

## Common Billing Issues

Customers may contact support about billing issues such as duplicate charges, failed payments, incorrect invoices, subscription fees, or unclear charges.

## Duplicate Charge

If a customer reports being charged twice:

1. Ask for the order number or transaction reference.
2. Confirm the payment date and amount.
3. Check whether one transaction is pending and one is completed.
4. If both charges are completed, escalate to the billing team for refund review.
5. Inform the customer that billing verification may take 3 to 5 business days.

## Failed Payment

If a customer reports a failed payment:

1. Ask the customer to confirm the payment method.
2. Check whether the payment gateway recorded a failed or declined transaction.
3. Ask the customer to verify card details, billing address, and available balance.
4. Suggest trying another payment method if the issue continues.

## Invoice Request

If a customer requests an invoice:

1. Confirm the customer email address.
2. Confirm the order number or subscription ID.
3. Send the invoice if available.
4. If the invoice is not available, escalate to the billing team.

## Suggested Support Tone

Use a calm and clear tone. Billing issues can be sensitive, so reassure the customer that the issue will be reviewed carefully.
