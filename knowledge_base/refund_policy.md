# Refund Policy

## Refund Request Handling

Customers may request a refund due to product issues, accidental purchases, duplicate charges, cancellation, or dissatisfaction.

## Refund Eligibility

A refund may be considered if:

1. The customer was charged incorrectly.
2. The product or service was not delivered.
3. The customer cancelled within the allowed refund window.
4. The issue was caused by a confirmed technical or billing error.

## Refund Review Process

1. Ask the customer for the order number.
2. Confirm the product or service purchased.
3. Check the purchase date.
4. Review whether the request falls within the refund policy.
5. Escalate to the refund or billing team if approval is required.

## Refund Timeline

Approved refunds may take 5 to 10 business days depending on the payment provider and bank processing time.

## Suggested Reply Guidance

Acknowledge the customer’s concern, explain that the request will be reviewed, and provide a realistic timeline. Avoid promising a refund before the case is verified.
