# Product Inquiry

This category covers questions about product features, specifications, compatibility, availability, pricing, setup, expected behavior, usage, or general product information.

Examples include:

- Is this product compatible with Windows?
- What features are included?
- Is this product available?
- How do I set it up?
- What are the technical specifications?
- How much does it cost?
- Can this product work with my device?

Do not use this category when the product is confirmed to be broken, malfunctioning, or producing errors. Those cases should normally be classified as technical issues.
