# Cancellation Policy

## Cancellation Request Handling

Customers may contact support to cancel an order, subscription, service, or account.

## Subscription Cancellation

If a customer wants to cancel a subscription:

1. Confirm the customer account email.
2. Confirm the subscription ID or order number.
3. Check whether the subscription is active.
4. Explain whether cancellation takes effect immediately or at the end of the billing cycle.
5. Confirm the cancellation once processed.

## Order Cancellation

If a customer wants to cancel an order:

1. Confirm the order number.
2. Check whether the order has already shipped or been fulfilled.
3. If the order has not shipped, cancel it if allowed.
4. If the order has shipped, explain the return or refund process.

## Important Notes

Do not confirm cancellation unless the cancellation has actually been processed. If additional approval is needed, tell the customer that the request has been forwarded to the relevant team.

## Suggested Support Tone

Be clear, direct, and reassuring. Customers requesting cancellation may be frustrated, so avoid defensive language.
